General information

Swissotel Büyük Efes is designed in a way to meet the needs of the guests who travel for both business and entertainment with 402 rooms meeting luxury and comfort. Property offers pleasant stay with gorgeous outdoor pool.

Providing service in region Alsancak of Izmir, Swissotel Buyuk Efes Izmir offers an ideal accommodation alternative both for business and touristic trips. You can have pleasant times in its outdoor and indoor pools of the property. You can relieve the tiredness of the entire year and refresh yourself thanks to the massage facilities. The property also offers hairdresser and car rental services. You can taste delicious delicacies in the restaurant and enjoy your time in the bar. The property is located 15 km to Adnan Menderes Airport and 5 km to the city centre.

Established to offer accommodation alternatives from all around the world, odamax.com provides online booking service with its approximately 600 thousand properties. Making thousands of properties in Turkey and across the world available for the users, the digital platform is on air with countless property alternatives to add comfort both to the business and touristic trips. Besides hotel chains and properties, Odamax.com offers many accommodation options for each budget and taste like boutique hotel, apart, hostels and is a rich source in the field of online hotel booking with its ever-increasing diversity of properties. The latest brand of Etstur, that is the leading tour operator in tourism sector in Turkey, Odamax.com offers the assurance of Etstur to its users everywhere in the world.

 

Bringingg many information that the users will need from room prices to the location of the property from special information like landmarks of the visited city to the availability status at a single point, Odamax.com has versions in Turkish, English, German and Russian now. However, different language options will be added in the upcoming period to provide convenience to the users. Odamax.com also offers the opportunity to make payment for the reservations both with credit card and during check-in at the hotel.

 

Digital platform also stands out with its user-friendly design. You can list the properties corresponding to your expectations by entering the destination and date range to the search box located on the home page of Odamax.com. If you wish to book for the property in a city you select, you can both perform the process as a Guest User and also you can sign up for free and login to do so. As a member, you can view your reservation details anytime you want and you can also cancel or change your reservation easily from Odamax.com web site.
